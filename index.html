J dilla

Bio

  • James Dewitt Yancey was born on Feb 7th, 1974 and passed away on Feb 10th, 2006.
  • Collaborated with Erykah Baduh, Busta Rhymes, Slum Village, Janet Jackson, and many more.

Style

  • His music contains a lot of swing and is unquantize.
  • Pioneered the sampling technique known as microchpping.
  • Hardware/Software of choice: Akai MPC

Flying Lotus

Bio

  • Steven Ellison was born on October 7th, 1983.
  • He has collaborated with Kendrick Lamar, Thundercat, Erykah Baduh, Snoop Dogg, Mac Miller, and Herbie Hancock.

Style

  • His sound is a combination of many genres dreamscape, hip-hop, soul, and chiptune.
  • He's influenced more by his enviornemt than his musical background which is included but not limited to Cartoon Network, Nintendo, and Marvel Comics.
  • Hardware/Software of choice: Flying Lotus being a more modern producer has a large supply of technology when making music this includes but is not limited to Akai MPC, Reason Studios, Ableton suite, and more.

Nujabes

Bio

  • Seba Jun was born on Febuary 7th, 1974 and passed away on Febuary 26th, 2010.
  • He Collaborated with Shing02, Fat Jon, Uyama Hiroto, Substantial, and Cise Starr
  • He also Composed the soundtrack for the anime "Samurai Champloo"

Style

  • His sound is a combination of hip-hop, jazz, and atmospheric soundscapes.
  • He used very soulful samples in away that puts the listener in a state of tranquility and peace.
  • Hardware/software of choice: Akai MPC.
